New in 2022!

Alana and Marko's forbidden new love in a
ancient world full of war and separation, sent them on their epic exploration for a new place
for their newborn to thrive away from the
Galactic War surrounding them.

Both soldiers fighting on opposites sides find love,
solace and a precious new goal.
Protect their baby girl.
Unknown in this new life,
Hazel was connected to both sides of the war
and is now being hunted across space and worlds unknown.

These comics are full of life,
from worlds you can not imagine and surprising characters who are rude,
horrifying,
sexy
and
just damn good hunters.

The adventure follows the little girl as she tells
the story of her parents
and growing up to learn how to fit into this World,
who will she learn from, who can protect her and who
she will have to hunt.

About the Authors

Writer

Brain K. Vaughan

He is known as an American comic book and television writer and after years of Saga adventure writen for our pleasure, the hard decision to have a hiatus in 2018 before releasing further Issue, must of been a tough decision. Due to the top releases of comic books and graphic novels coming on to the scene in 2018, it was decided to post-pone the release of Saga issues 55-60 untill now.

His other work includes:

  • Creater of Ex machina comic book series along side Tony Harric.
  • Executive producer of the TV series Under the Dome.
  • Creater of Paper Girls comic book series along side Cliff Chiang.
  • Writer for Pride of Baghdad comic book series.
  • Runaways superhero comic book series created along side Adrian Alphona

Artist

Fiona Staples

Deciding along side the writer of the Saga comic book series, that a hiatus, was in the best interest of it's release, she fills our vision of new characters once again. The Saga series might follow a family and their young daughter but that doesn't mean the imagary holds back in anyway. Violence, gore, pleasure and pain can be seen throughout her illustrations bringing the emotions of the quests of these characters to life.

Her illustrations can be seen, along side others, for pieces of work including:

  • Archie comics
  • T.H.U.N.D.E.R. Agents
  • Trick 'r Treat
She has won many awards for her illustrations in the Saga Series.
